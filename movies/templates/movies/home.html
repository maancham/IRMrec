Welcome to the Information Retrieval Movie Recommendation!

Thank you for participating in this study. We are working to build the first test collection for offline evaluation of recommender systems that follows the best practices of information retrieval. As you have seen in the tutorial video, our website consists of three main parts. You can access each part from the dropdown menu in the top right corner of the page:
1. Judge Movies: Based on your rating profile, you will be recommended a set of movies. For each movie, there are a few questions that you need to answer.
2. View Judged Movies: You can always review the movies you have already judged in the system. You can even modify your answers by clicking on the movie title.
3. Final Ranking: In the last step, we will ask you to choose your top three movies from all the recommendations. Please note that this step will only be unlocked once you have judged all the recommended movies.
